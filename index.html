Blood Donation Saves Lives

Why Donate Blood?

Every blood donation can save up to 3 lives. Blood is needed every 2 seconds for patients in emergencies, surgeries, and chronic illnesses.

Donation Requirements

  • Age 17-65 years
  • Weight at least 50kg
  • Good health condition
  • No recent tattoos/piercings

Donation Process

  1. Registration
  2. Health check
  3. Donation (10-15 mins)
  4. Refreshments

About Us

Our Mission

Pakistan Blood Donors Network connects blood donors with recipients across Pakistan, creating a reliable platform that saves lives through voluntary donations.

How We Work

We maintain a nationwide database of voluntary donors. When someone needs blood, our system quickly finds matching donors in their area.

Our Impact

We've facilitated thousands of successful donations, helping patients in emergencies, surgeries, and chronic conditions nationwide.

Our Services

Donor Registration

We register voluntary blood donors across Pakistan with their availability and blood types.

Donor Matching

Our search system helps patients find compatible donors by blood type and location.

Donation Campaigns

We organize blood donation camps with hospitals and NGOs nationwide.
